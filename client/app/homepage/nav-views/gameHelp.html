Rules of the Game!


  • Three players are needed for a game. There will always be a bot as the fourth player.
  • In a game, a player can either be the "guesser" or part of the "panel".
  • A game consists of three rounds where each player gets a turn to be a guesser.
  • The winner of the game is determined at the end of the game as whoever has the highest score.

Guesser

  • As the guesser, your first task is to ask the panel a question (the bot is amongst the panel).
  • Upon receiving the panel's response to your question, your objective is to guess which response might be the bot's response.
  • If you guess the bot's response correctly, you win the round!

Panel

  • As the panel, upon receiving the guesser's question, you will also know what the bot's response to the question will be
  • Winning the round for you will be to have the guesser pick your response as their answer.
  • In the event that your answer was not chosen, but that the guesser still picked the incorrect answer, you will still "win" the round as part of the panel
  • Knowing the bot's answer before providing your own response to the question gives you an advantage. Use it wisely!

Game Mechanics!


Scoring


  • If guesser chooses the bot's response correctly, the guesser receives 30 points and the panel receives 0.
  • Otherwise, 2 of out the 3 panel players will receive 10 points for fooling the guesser while the guesser receives 0.
  • The panel player whose answer resulted in the guesser selecting their response will receive 15 points instead of the 10 points.

Overall Winner and Round Winner

  • Overall winner is decided after a round-robin.
  • The player with the most points after the round-robin will be selected as the winner.
  • Round winner is split between either the guesser or the entire panel.

Achievements

  • Include the number of times the player guessed the bot correctly 5 times.
  • Includes a marker for when a player achieves their first 100 points.
  • Includes an achievement when the player gets their first 5 wins.
  • Includes an achievement when the player plays their first 10 games
